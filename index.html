About Project ATL

Author

aninointhenight — hobbyist transit mapper, Antipolo City.

Goals

Document and map all 124 TODAs (Tricycle Operators and Drivers Associations) in Antipolo City, Philippines. Long-term: build a commuter app starting from Metro Manila.

Data Sources

  • Antipolo City PTRB (Public Transport Regulatory Board) — primary source
  • 2024–2025 Antipolo City Facts & Figures
  • OpenStreetMap, Google Maps, Wikimapia
  • Facebook, Reddit, YouTube
  • Direct field observation (Strava, street view)

References / Credits

  • PTRB Antipolo — official TODA registry, March 2025
  • LTFRB — national transport authority (limited jurisdiction over tricycles)
  • OTC (Office of Transport Cooperatives) — cooperative registry
  • CartoDB Positron tiles — map base layer
  • Leaflet.js — mapping library
  • Supabase — backend / auth

Project ATL welcomes community contributions! If you know of a TODA terminal that isn't on the map yet, or have updated information about an existing one, you can submit it for review.

How it works

  • Click any confirmed/draft pin and tap "Suggest Edit / Add Info"
  • Fill in the details you know — partial info is fine
  • Your submission goes to the admin for review
  • Once approved, it appears on the map for everyone

Guidelines

  • Only submit terminals in Antipolo City (for now)
  • Do not submit personal information
  • One submission per minute
  • Be as accurate as possible — wrong pins cause confusion
